## Step 3. 계산식

- Gross Profit = 구매자 수 × Average Price × Gross Margin
- Discount Cost = 구매자 수 × Average Price × Discount Rate
- Net Profit = Gross Profit − Discount Cost − Campaign Cost
- Incremental Profit = Treatment Net Profit − Control Net Profit
